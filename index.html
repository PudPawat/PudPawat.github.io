Who I Am

I'm a PhD-trained AI engineer currently working as a Technical Consultant Engineer II at Appier — Taiwan's largest digital marketing SaaS — where I architect enterprise-grade AI systems deployed across Southeast Asia.

My work sits at the intersection of agentic AI systems, cloud-native data engineering, and enterprise software deployment. I design LLM-powered agents with RAG pipelines, Chain-of-Thought reasoning, and MCP-wrapped APIs — systems that actually work in production.

Before Appier, I spent five years in academia and industry roles spanning computer vision, robotics, E-bike AI systems, and AOI quality control. I hold patents in both Europe and Taiwan, have published in IEEE and MDPI journals, and was invited as an adjunct lecturer at my undergraduate alma mater.

I believe the best AI work bridges deep research with practical engineering — and I've spent my career doing exactly that.
